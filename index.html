The Big Picture

The dark ages are over and JavaScript is here to stay. Thanks to the browser wars all the major players in the web industry have JavaScript engines which offer excellent performance and exciting APIs.

Still, some of JavaScript's syntactical (curly braces & semicolons!) and semantical features are lacking behind languages we learned to embrace (python, ruby, haskell).

Fortunately we have CoffeeScript.
